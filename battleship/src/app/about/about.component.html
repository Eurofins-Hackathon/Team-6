About Battleship Game

Welcome to the Battleship game! This is a turn-based strategy game where players take turns attacking each other's boards to sink their opponent's ships.

How to Play

  • Choose a game mode: Player vs AI or Player vs Player.
  • Place your ships on the board by dragging and dropping them.
  • Take turns attacking your opponent's board.
  • The game ends when all ships on one board are sunk.

Features

  • Drag-and-drop ship placement.
  • Visual feedback for hits and misses.
  • Basic AI for single-player mode.
